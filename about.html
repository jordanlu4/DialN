About DialN

DialN is more than a study tool, it's a cosmic journey designed to elevate your focus and ignite your imagination. Through its subtle space-themed illustrations and minimalist design, it minimizes distractions and transforms study sessions into adventures. With customizable timers and ambient sounds, DialN creates an ideal environment for concentration, making learning an engaging and productive experience. Embark on your educational voyage with DialN and explore the universe of knowledge with every study session.

About The Creator

My name is Jordan Lu and I'm currently an undergraduate student studying computer science at the University of Colorado Boulder. I created this application as solution a personal issue. I like to time my study session and work in intervals. Using a timer app on your phone is a simple and effective way of doing so, but I wanted to create something more engaging and creative.
